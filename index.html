Course Details

Learning Objectives
  • Compare and contrast Common Law and Civil Law systems through practical case analysis
  • Master key terminology for American courtroom procedures and roles
  • Participate actively in adversarial trial simulation
  • Communicate effectively about legal proceedings in English
Course Specifications
  • Level: B2-C1 English proficiency
  • Students: French law students (up to 20)
  • Duration: 120 minutes
  • Format: Interactive simulation with role-playing
  • Languages: Bilingual (English/French) materials
